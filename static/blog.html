Why AI Visibility Matters

AI Rank · 2025

When users ask ChatGPT, Claude, or other AI assistants for recommendations, they trust the suggestions. If your product isn't in those responses, you're invisible to a growing share of discoverability.

AI visibility isn't traditional SEO. It's about how well your product matches the kinds of queries people phrase when talking to AI — "best free X", "online tools for Y", "alternatives to Z". Understanding which phrases surface your site is the first step to improving.
